Welcome to the Salmon Cookie Store!

Our Story

Pat first came up with the idea for baking and selling cookies while still in primary school, making snacks for the neighborhood kids (and families) in between weekend fishing trips with his father off Westport or Astoria. His love of salmon, and cookies, continues to this day, and he wants to share it with you!

Drop by one of our locations for a sample. Enjoy a cup of coffee while you are there. Bring home a box of salmon coookies for you and your family to enjoy or purchase online and have salmon cookies delivered to your doorstep!

Meet the Saalmons

The Saalmons are kneading the salmon-cookie-dough just for you, everyday. The dough, the fish, and the frosting ingredients are procured with you in mind, and baked to perfection using a decades-old Saalmon recipe. Our bakers and customer-oriented staff are the Saalmon family!
